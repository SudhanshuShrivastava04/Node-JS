What is Versioning?

For example :- version : 1.3.4

first part -> 1
second part -> 3
third part -> 4


// 3rd part (Last part) - Minor fixes (Optional) 
Here the last part is (Latest) -> 4

// 2nd part (middle part) - Recommended Bug fix (Security fix)
Here the middle part is (Latest) -> 3

// 1st part (first part) - Major/Breaking Update (Latest Release)
Here the first part is (Latest) -> 1
